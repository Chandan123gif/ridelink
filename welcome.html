About Us

Ridelink is an experienced and dynamic e-logistics company that was founded in 2017 in Uganda and launched in 2022. Since then, we have been growing rapidly, expanding our operations throughout East and Central Africa, and earning a reputation as a reliable and trusted logistics partner for businesses across various sectors.

Mission

To provide innovative and reliable logistics solutions to businesses across various sectors, leveraging our expertise, technology, and extensive network of partners to optimize our clients’ supply chain and drive their success

Vission

To be the top logistics company in Africa, providing exceptional services, driving economic growth, and leveraging technology to innovate and deliver cost-effective solutions.”

Goals

To be a leader in creating sustainable transport and logistics solutions tailored to grow business on the continentTo promote a mutual commitment of growth towards the businesses and lives of all Ridelink partners and stakeholders. To unlock the untapped potential of networking businesses across the continent. To create customer satisfaction by offering businesses dependability and professionalism in transportation. To continuously improve our internal systems and technology so they can be well equipped to provide a reliable service to customers.
